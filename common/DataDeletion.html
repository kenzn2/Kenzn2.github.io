Data Deletion Policy
For Kenzn2 Projects

Your data privacy and deletion rights with Kenzn2 applications and services.

#data-deletion-policy

1. Data Deletion Overview

At Kenzn2, we respect your right to control your personal data. This Data Deletion Policy explains how you can request the deletion of your data from our applications and services, including Discord bots, Chrome extensions, and other AI/ML applications.

2. What Data May Be Collected

Depending on which services you use, we may collect different types of data:

  • Discord Bots: User IDs, server IDs, command usage statistics, user preferences and settings, message content for AI processing (temporary)
  • Chrome Extensions: Usage analytics and preferences, extension settings and configurations, performance metrics
  • AI/ML Applications: Input data for processing, generated outputs and results, usage patterns and analytics, user feedback and ratings

3. Your Data Deletion Rights

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data under various privacy laws including GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable regulations. We provide multiple ways to exercise this right.

4. How to Request Data Deletion

To request data deletion, you can contact us through:

  • Email: Send a data deletion request to Antoan0105@gmail.com with your user information
  • Contact Form: Use the contact form below to submit your deletion request

5. Data Deletion Process

Our data deletion process follows these steps:

  • Request Received: We acknowledge your deletion request within 48 hours
  • Identity Verification: We may require verification to ensure data protection
  • Data Location: We identify all locations where your data is stored
  • Deletion Execution: Data is permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days
  • Confirmation: You receive confirmation once deletion is complete

6. What Data Cannot Be Deleted

Some data may be retained for legal or technical reasons:

  • Data required to be retained by law
  • Anonymous or aggregated data that cannot identify you
  • Data necessary for fraud prevention or security purposes
  • Backup data that will be deleted in the normal course of operations

7. Timeline for Deletion

We aim to complete data deletion requests as quickly as possible:

  • Immediate: Active data from our primary systems
  • Within 7 days: Data from our application databases
  • Within 30 days: Data from backup systems and logs
  • Within 90 days: Archived data and disaster recovery systems

8. Third-Party Services

Some of our services use third-party platforms:

  • Discord: Bot data is stored on Discord's servers - you may need to request deletion directly from Discord
  • Chrome Web Store: Extension usage data may be subject to Google's policies
  • Cloud Providers: We use cloud services that may have their own retention policies
