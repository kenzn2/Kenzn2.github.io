Data Deletion Policy

Your data privacy and deletion rights with Kenzn2 applications and services

Data Deletion Overview

At Kenzn2, we respect your right to control your personal data. This Data Deletion Policy explains how you can request the deletion of your data from our applications and services, including Discord bots, Chrome extensions, and other AI/ML applications.

What Data May Be Collected

Discord Bots

  • User IDs and server IDs
  • Command usage statistics
  • User preferences and settings
  • Message content for AI processing (temporary)

Chrome Extensions

  • Usage analytics and preferences
  • Extension settings and configurations
  • Performance metrics

AI/ML Applications

  • Input data for processing
  • Generated outputs and results
  • Usage patterns and analytics
  • User feedback and ratings

Important Information

Data Retention Exceptions

Some data may be retained for legal compliance, security purposes, or legitimate business interests as permitted by applicable laws. This includes:

  • Legal obligations and regulatory requirements
  • Security and fraud prevention
  • Backup systems (automatically purged within 90 days)
  • Anonymous usage analytics that cannot identify individuals

Service Impact

Deleting your data may impact your ability to use certain features or services. Some consequences include:

  • Loss of personalized settings and preferences
  • Inability to recover previous interactions or history
  • Reset of usage statistics and achievements
  • Potential service limitations or restrictions
